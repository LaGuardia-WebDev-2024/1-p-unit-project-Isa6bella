Moo Deng

Who is This Diva?

Moo Deng, a baby hippo born to mother Joanne Deng.

She currently resides in Khao Kheow Open Zoo in Si Racha, Chonburi, Thailand.

Why should you care?

  • She is very sassy and pink
  • She's a pretty lady with cute ears
  • She is a Pygmy Hippopotamus
  • Moo-Deng means 'bouncy pork'
    in Thai. I think this name suits her perfectly.


Precious Moo Deng eating assorted fruits and plants with her Mom, Joanne.

She gained popularity in Thailand through social media clips of her trying to bite zoo keepers.
Her fame quickly grew on Western social media and all over.
The world cannot get enough of this baby hippo!

In the wild, Pygmy Hippopotamus (Moo Deng's Species) prefer to live alone,
and even avoid eachother all together.
